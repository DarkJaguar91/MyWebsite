Glossary

  • KATA - is a Japanese word describing detailed choreographed patterns of movements practised either solo or in pairs.
  • Komite - is the sport fighting match in many martial art sports.
  • Unison KATA - KATA performed by 3 or more people (usually 3) at the same time, and in synchronous.
